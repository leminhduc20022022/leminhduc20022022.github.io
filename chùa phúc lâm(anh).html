Phuc Lam Pagoda is located in La Mat village, Phu Ung commune, An Thi district, Hung Yen province, about 1 hour away from Hanoi. Phuc Lam Pagoda is a spiritual tourist destination that attracts tourists with its peaceful and tranquil village landscape. Coming to Phuc Lam Pagoda in Hung Yen, visitors can not only admire the beautiful scenery, freely check-in virtual life, but also pray for luck and fortune at the beginning of the year at this sacred temple. Besides, visitors can also immerse themselves in the fresh and peaceful atmosphere of the scenery here to feel relief in their hearts. Phuc Lam Pagoda has a long history, belongs to a small district of Hung Yen province. This temple is a spiritual destination for people in the region and has recently become a destination attracting many tourists from all over the world with its unique and impressive architecture, dubbed the "Thai golden temple" of Vietnam. Phuc Lam Hung Yen Pagoda attracts tourists with its "golden" appearance, visiting this temple, visitors will admire the captivating scenery in the spiritual place. The pagoda consists of 4 towers, has many large Buddha statues and has a sacred, mysterious appearance without any purity.
