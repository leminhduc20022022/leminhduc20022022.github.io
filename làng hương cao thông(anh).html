Located about 40 km from Hanoi, Cao Thon incense village in Bao Khe commune, Hung Yen city, Hung Yen province, is one of the largest incense making villages in the country. Cao Thon's incense is famous for its quality and distinct scent, favored by the domestic market and exported to a number of countries. If you have the opportunity to come to Hung Yen, visit this interesting tourist destination in Hung Yen!
