Ung Temple Festival

Back Vietnamese

Ung temple festival is held on the 11th day of the first lunar month every year, in Phu Ung commune, An Thi district. This is a festival to commemorate General Pham Ngu Lao. Ung Temple worships General Pham Ngu Lao, a famous general of the Tran Dynasty, who made great contributions to the resistance war against the invading Yuan - Mongols in the 13th century. Pham Ngu Lao was born and raised in Phu Ung village, Duong Hao district, Phu Thuong Hong district (now Phu Ung commune, An Thi district) from an early age, he showed an unusual temperament, an upright character. Up to now, the story of General Pham Ngu Lao is still told by the people as a strange story about a heroic general with patriotism, deep hatred for the enemy, extraordinary health and courage. Legend has it that when he was 13 years old, there was someone in the village who passed the position of mandarin, held a banquet to treat the villagers, the whole village came to attend, only he did not. The mother asked why he didn't come. He replied that he had to make a name for himself as a boy, but he couldn't do anything, so he felt ashamed. Every day, Pham Ngu Lao still sat on the side of the road, chopping bamboo. , chamfering, knitting baskets. One day, Hung Dao Vuong Tran Quoc Tuan went from Van Kiep (Chi Linh) to the capital Thang Long. Soldiers guarding Tran Hung Dao went ahead to clear the road, saw Pham Ngu Lao, shouted at him, still sat quietly as if he didn't hear anything, the soldiers stabbed their thighs with spears, bleeding profusely, he still did not move. Tran Hung Dao found it strange and went to ask the beginning and the end of the matter. When he learned about Pham Ngu Lao, because he was so busy thinking about the country that he forgot about his physical pain, Hung Dao Vuong realized that this was a talent of the country, so he summoned him to take advantage. With the innate talent and forging pair of Tran Quoc Tuan Pham Ngu Lao quickly became an excellent general who twice defeated the invaders Nguyen - Mong. In memory of a talented general who is a son. of the homeland, the villagers of Phu Ung built a temple to worship Pham Ngu Lao right on the old ground of his house. The festival opens with the procession of Princess Tinh Hue (Daughter of Pham Ngu Lao) from the royal palace to Pham Tien Cong mausoleum to present to him and to Pham Ngu Lao temple to present to his father. During the procession, thousands of people and guests from all over the world squeezed together under the palanquin with the thought that their wishes in the new year would come true. The ceremony is held solemnly with traditional rituals such as great ceremony, internal sacrifice, external ceremony... The Hoi section with folk games, sports, and fun was devised by General Pham Ngu Lao to train the soldiers' health and will, such as a wrestling contest. Round tick, made of square wood painted red. The playing field is a large yard divided into two sides, east and west, in the middle of the field, a horizontal line is drawn, in the middle of the line, a hole is dug for an oil, at the two ends of the field, a hole is dug on each side. Each team has 8 cards and 1 total, their heads are covered with scarves and loincloths. Each team wears a different colored loincloth. Before the game, two teams line up in two rows to worship in front of the temple. The referee holds the ball in the hole in the middle of the court, gives the signal, the two sides fight to steal it into the opponent's hole, whoever puts it in the other's hole is the winner. During the festival days, besides the ceremonial parts, there are friendly sports activities such as badminton, volleyball, cultural activities for early spring, singing quan ho, singing military drums, etc. Ung Temple Festival is held from January 11 to 13, attracting thousands of visitors from all over the world to offer incense and celebrate, is one of the major festivals, opening for cultural activities and festivals.
