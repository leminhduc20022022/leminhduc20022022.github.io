Day cake in Gau village is a delicious dish in Hung Yen that has been passed down by many people for generations. This cake is made from sticky rice with yellow flowers, soaked and washed thoroughly. After preliminary processing, the baker will cook the sticky rice until cooked. The inside of the cake is the part of green beans that are blown and pounded and mixed with a little sugar. For savory cakes, replace the bean paste with lean pork. Biting a piece of the cake, diners will feel the sweet and fragrant taste of the beans combined with the fragrant and flexible taste of glutinous rice outside, which will bring a real taste of the dish. unforgettableness. If you have the opportunity to travel to Hung Yen, you can also buy this cake as a gift for relatives and friends.
