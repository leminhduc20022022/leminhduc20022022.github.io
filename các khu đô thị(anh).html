1 LAC HONG URBAN AREA

Lac Hong urban area is a project of worker housing, commercial housing serving the living and living needs of workers and people of Pho Noi in particular and My Hao district in general. The project is planned by Lac Hong Investment Joint Stock Company on a land area of 151,361m2 in the territory of Nhan Hoa commune, My Hao district, Hung Yen province.

The commercial housing area of the project is arranged in the center of the land, creating a fresh and convenient living space for residents of the residential areas. Meanwhile, the living quarters for workers, including a complex of 5-storey buildings, service, sports and green buildings are arranged in the east of the land.

– Project name: Lac Hong Phuc urban area – Investor: Lac Hong Investment Joint Stock Company – Type of development: New urban area – Location: Pho Noi, Nhan Hoa Commune, My Hao District, Hung Yen – Scale: 151,361m2 – Residential land: 79,752m2 of which: + Adjacent residential land: 38,052m2 + Residential land for workers: 23,624m2 + Mixed high-rise land: 7,786m2 + Villa land: 10.29m2 + Public land: 4,326m2 + Land for cultural houses: 844m2 + School land: 2.26m2 + Project administration: 1,222m2 + Land for traffic: 51,913m2 + Trees, playground: 14,939m2 + Area main road: 15.81m2 + Internal traffic, parking: 36,103m2

3 V-GREEN CITY STREET

V-Green City Pho Noi Hung Yen New Urban Area project has a scale of more than 100 hectares located at the northern gateway of Hung Yen province with synchronous infrastructure and landscape planning. The project is expected to become a bustling and livable model city of Hung Yen province in the future when the Hanoi - Hai Phong highway is fully connected.

V-GreenCity is known as an ecological area with a favorable location in the center of My Hao town, located at Km 33, new road 39A of Yen My district, Pho Noi, Hung Yen province. The project is only about 30 minutes from Hanoi by car, about 12km from Ecopark New Urban Area…

The project has completed basic infrastructure such as electricity, roads, green tree system, lake, .... In addition, V-green City Pho Noi is fully invested with necessary facilities to meet the high demands of residents here such as: lake, park, commercial center, gym, gym. Sports, schools, hospitals, football fields... can be immediately handed over to customers for construction.
