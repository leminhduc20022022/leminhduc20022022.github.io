Surely we all want to have a great vacation with our family in a beautiful natural land, accompanied by modern entertainment games to dispel tired worries. If so, come to Ecopark Hung Yen, one of the famous amusement parks that is extremely suitable for picnic activities and family bonding.

Introduction of ecopark Hung Yen urban area

Ecopark Hung Yen is located in Van Giang district of Hung Yen province, less than 25km from the center of Hanoi. Ecopark is located next to Vinhomes Ocean Park Gia Lam urban area, next to Bat Trang pottery village and Gia Lam district. This place owns a large-scale green ecosystem with vast trees, green and lively lakes. The surroundings are very fresh and close to nature. Besides, built on the terrain surrounded by 2 rivers Red and Duong, so the atmosphere is also extremely cool and pleasant, very suitable for tourists who want to find a place to rest in the fresh air. away from the smog of the city.

How to have a picnic at Ecopark

Visitors can use personal vehicles such as motorbikes or cars to move in 2 routes: (see address) Route 1: follow the direction of Ring Road 3 to Thanh Tri bridge, when crossing the bridge, turn right, continue for about 4.3km to reach Ecopark. There are many signposts along the way, so you can rest assured. Route 2: go to Chuong Duong or Vinh Tuy bridge. After crossing the bridge, you go along the Bat Trang dike road to the foot of Thanh Tri bridge, then go to the Hanoi - Hung Yen intercity road about 4.2km and you will arrive at Ecopark urban area. Travel time: about 40-45 minutes.

Explore Ecopark Summer Park

What does Ecopark have? If you still have this question, come to Ecopark Summer Park, located at Rung Co apartment complex and Vuon Tung villa area in Ecopark green city. Ecopark Summer Park has an area of ​​up to 2.5 hectares, suitable for picnic activities, fun, team-building or outdoor BBQ. With a fresh space covered by extremely fresh and beautiful gardens, Summer Park will definitely be an attractive destination for families with many young children.

Cafe Starbuck Ecopark

When coming to Summer Park, you will not be able to ignore one of the biggest coffee brands in the world - Starbucks. On January 9, 2021, Starbucks Ecopark Cafe was officially opened and has become one of the cafes with the most beautiful view in Vietnam. With a unique coffee flavor, this place will be a worthy stop for you to sit back and relax after hours of hard work.

Sand play area

The sand play area at Ecopark Hung Yen is very large. Inside there are interesting games for children such as slides, grab bars, sand baths, etc., which are very suitable for parents to let their children participate in activities to improve their health. Parents can have private time with each other, letting their children play in the sand play area.

Play on the lawn

Referring to Summer Park at Ecopark, it is impossible to ignore the extremely large and beautiful lawn. This is an ideal place for families to perform activities such as kicking a ball, throwing discs or riding a downhill car, team building, picnic, etc.

Flower truss road

A check-in location that many people take pictures here. This stretch of road is located right next to the lawn in the park and is easy to recognize from a distance because of the vibrant colors of the flowers.

Stilt house

Ecopark Summer Park has a rather unique stilt house where you can sit and rest below. If you want to use the stilt house, please remember to contact the management.

Lake and pond

Another interesting check-in point at Ecopark Summer Park is the Lake located in the central park area. Next to the lake is a beautiful bamboo grove, extremely suitable for photography enthusiasts.

Kolorado's amusement park

The Kolorado home play area, next to the sand playground, is also an extremely interesting playground built specifically for children. This is a modern invested amusement park with many attractive games for children such as ball house, climbing, slide or folk games, etc. Here, children can participate in fun activities such as ball house, painting statues, shoveling cassava seeds, climbing, etc., both having fun and having the opportunity to exercise their bodies and increase flexibility. Ticket price for one child with an accompanying parent is 70000.

At Ecopark ecological urban area, there are full of food shops from popular to high-class, from the north to the south. All those luxury restaurants are located in Pho Truc Ecopark. OTHER Amusement Area In ECOPARK

1. Swan Lake Ecopark

Swan Lake Park is the focal point of Ecopark urban area, a must-visit destination for Ecopark tourists. With a large space in harmony with nature, the clear and joyful chirping of birds along with the white storks flying freely in the sky, and especially the beautiful flock of swans swimming in the middle of the lake, this place It will definitely give visitors a feeling of connection with nature and peace of mind. Some activities visitors can do such as cycling around, running, walking, flying kites or can kayaking and taking pictures to check-in with the surrounding scenery.

2. Ecopark Korean Walking Street Coming Soon

Korean Walking Street in Ecopark, expected to come into operation in 2022, when Sky Oasis and Solforest apartments are handed over. Korean Walking Street will promise to be the center of entertainment and tourism in Ecopark. Modeled after the busy commercial pedestrian streets in Korea such as Itaewon, Hongdea, Garosu-Gil, the Korean pedestrian street will have many subdivisions such as: food street, art check-in street, shopping street , fantasy street, entertainment street...

3. Swimming pools at Ecopark

In addition, if you go with a family with young children and the weather is not too cold, the little ones will love to play in the swimming pool. At each subdivision of Ecopark, there is a swimming pool to serve residents here.

4. Bat Trang Pottery Village

Bat Trang pottery village is located right next to Ecopark green city. It only takes 5-10 minutes by car or taxi to get here. Coming to Bat Trang, you can explore Bat Trang with its unique ancient architecture, experience painting by hand in real pottery workshops or shop for lovely porcelain souvenirs at Bat Trang Pottery Market. This is also an opportunity for you to learn more about a very beautiful traditional culture of Ha Thanh.

5. Road around Ecopark Grand Island Villa

The road around the island villa Ecopark Grand is also a place that tourists should experience. When walking or cycling here, you can admire a beautiful road with cool natural scenery and a system of canals like in the Southwest region. In addition, there are also a number of cafes nearby as a stopover. Visitors can enjoy sweeping views of the green forest and sparkling lake surface of the luxury subdivision Ecopark Island Villas. The beautiful scenery is also very suitable for you to take selfies with your family.
